Seager Marine is your home on the water

Enjoy a full season on the water with the convenience of a dedicated slip through Seager Marine. Their seasonal slips accommodate boats up to 24 feet in length, giving boaters easy access to launch, dock, and enjoy more time on the water without the hassle of trailering every trip. Slip holders benefit from a well-managed marina environment and a team that understands how to support a smooth, stress-free boating season..

Priority is given to customers who purchase their boats through Seager Marine, helping owners enjoy a seamless experience from purchase to dock.
