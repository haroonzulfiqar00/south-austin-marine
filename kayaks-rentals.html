Rules and Policies for Kayak and Paddleboard Rentals

  • All participants are required to wear life jackets at all times.
  • We ask all paddlers to remain within the bay during your rental. Rentals are not permitted beyond the bay or out on Lake Ontario.
  • Do not enter active boat channels or swim areas near Kershaw Park.
  • Remain mindful of boat traffic at all times.
  • All equipment must be returned on time; additional charges apply for late returns.
  • No alcohol consumption is permitted while using kayaks or paddleboards.
  • Fishing is not permitted from kayaks or paddleboards.

Cancelation policy

Must provide 7 days notice or forfeit deposit.

Incliment weather policy

Winds exceeding 15 mph or storms present though durations Inclement weather will be decided day of and not before.
