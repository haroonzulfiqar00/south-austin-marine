WELCOME TO SEAGER MARINE

We Are Western NY’s Friendly Full Service Marina!

Seager Marine provides the highest quality boat sales, service, rentals and storage for the Finger Lakes and Greater Rochester area. Conveniently located on the Canandaigua City Pier, Seager Marine has proudly provided a full service marine experience including new and used boat sales for 53 years. We are proud to carry boats from world class manufacturers of Nautique, Scout Boats, Avalon Pontoon Boats, Lund Fishing Boats, Honda®, Mercury and Yamaha Marine Engines.
