A Lakefront Experience

The Canandaigua East Lake Road is a full-service waterfront destination offering both on-the-water fun and professional marine care. This location provides expert fiberglass and gelcoat repair, detailing, upholstery, and canvas services to keep boats looking and performing their best. Guests can also enjoy kayak and stand-up paddleboard rentals, boat rentals for easy lake access, and a well-stocked Pro Shop featuring watersports gear, boards, tubes, apparel, and lake-day essentials — making it a convenient stop for both boating needs and recreation on Canandaigua Lake.
