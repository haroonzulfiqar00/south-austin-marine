Winterization & Spring Start-up

Seager Marine’s winterization and spring start-up services help protect your boat through the off-season and ensure it’s ready to perform when boating weather returns. Their technicians properly winterize engines, fuel systems, plumbing, and onboard components to guard against freeze damage and corrosion, giving owners peace of mind during storage. When spring arrives, the team performs thorough inspections, system checks, and start-up procedures so your boat launches smoothly, safely, and without delays — getting you back on the water with confidence.
