Your Local Home for Life on the Water

The Sales and Showroom location at Seager Marine is a full-service boating destination offering a wide selection of new boats from trusted brands including Nautique, Scout, NauticStar, Avalon Pontoons, Revel Pontoons, and Lund, along with Mercury and Honda power. In addition to expert boat sales, this location features a complete service department with certified technicians specializing in inboard, outboard, and interior work, plus convenient indoor and outdoor storage options. Customers also have access to RGC docks and hoists, as well as a fully stocked parts department, making it a one-stop shop for sales, service, storage, and long-term boat ownership support.

Service Center
