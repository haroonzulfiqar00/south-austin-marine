Your Full-Service Keuka Lake Boating Destination

Seager Marine’s Canandaigua/Keuka Lake location at East Bluff Seager on Keuka Lake offers a full suite of marine services and amenities for boating enthusiasts. This location provides boat sales featuring the same popular brands as their Canandaigua showroom, along with comprehensive service and repair, parts, and custom upholstery and canvas work. Customers can take advantage of convenient boat rentals, wet and dry slip rentals, and storage options. The location also sells RGC hoists and docks and includes a house rental property, making it a versatile destination for both boaters and vacationers looking to enjoy the beauty of Keuka Lake.
