More Ways to Enjoy the Water

The Canandaigua Marina is a vibrant waterfront destination offering multiple ways to enjoy time on the water. This location serves as the hub for boat rentals and private charters, kayak and stand-up paddleboard rentals, and is the main home of Freedom Boat Club, making boating accessible for locals and visitors alike. Guests can also enjoy an on-site restaurant and event space, creating a lively atmosphere both on and off the water. In addition, the marina offers slip rentals with wet and quasi-dry docking options, and allows customers to keep boats on trailers at the property, with convenient self-launch access available at the nearby public launch.
