Before You Launch: Rental Guidelines

Boat Rentals Insurance is required and built into your booking fees... please read below!

Kindly be advised that each rental reservation made with us includes the purchase of additional insurance through a third party. This cost-effective measure is designed to offer you peace of mind during your rental experience. The insurance policy has been established to ensure your safety and provide a worry-free experience in the event of unforeseen circumstances while on the water. Following your reservation, an email will guide you through the coverage details, safeguarding you against unforeseen costly incidents and securing your time spent creating memories.

  • Tubes, surf boards, wake boards, tow ropes and sports life jackets available for rent to make your experience even more unforgettable!
  • Boat rental insurance is required and included in your booking cost.
  • Boat rentals are full day or multi-day. Multi-day rentals can be kept at a cottage with approval or can be returned to Seager Marine each evening prior to sunset.
  • All pictures of rental boats are stock photos and representative of the boats we offer.
  • Full day rentals start are $550 plus taxes. Price does not includes gas. Multi-day boat renters responsible for any fuel used during their trip.
  • Never driven a boat before and looking for a driver or just want to enjoy yourself and not have to worry about driving?
