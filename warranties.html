Common Warranty Questions

Q.
Can I purchase a warranty for a used boat?
A.
Absolutely! You can add a warranty on your boat's accessory components and you can also add a separate warranty on your boat's engine.
Q.
Do I need to buy my boat or engine from Seager Marine to purchase a warranty?
A.
No! We are happy to help you figure out which warranty is right for you, even if you did not purchase your boat or engine at Seager Marine.
Q.
Is there anything that covers my trailer?
A.
Yes, we have roadside assistance for your trailer, which also covers the vehicle towing the trailer.
Q.
What types of engines are covered?
A.
You can get an outboard, inboard, stern drive, diesel and personal watercraft engine covered.
Q.
My Mercury engine comes with a 3 year warranty. Can I add additional time?
A.
Yes! You can add up to 5 years and we are happy to help facilitate that with Mercury.
